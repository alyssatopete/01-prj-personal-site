About Me

My journey into the world of design began in 2019, prompted by the challenges of the pandemic. Despite my initial lack of drawing or designing skills from my sport focused background, I set a goal to master digital art & graphics design within that year. Through dedicated learning of Adobe Suite and ClipStudio Paint, I discovered my love for the creative process. The journey was demanding, but the rewards were immeasurable. I firmly believe that designing for others is a powerful form of expression, reflecting emotions and facilitating communication. My approach to design seamlessly blends aesthetics with usability, ensuring that my creations are not only visually captivating but also effortlessly navigable.
